About FundZ

Funding on FUNDZ is free, with a 0% platform fee. The 0\% platform fee option empowers you to receive maximum funds for your funding campaign.
On our Online Crowdfunding Platform, you can raise funds for:

  • Medical fundraisers such as cancer treatment, Kidney Failure, Heart Diseases & SMA (Spinal Muscular Atrophy).
  • Sports funding for sports equipment for better training and all.
  • Education fundraisers such sponsorship educational, out of india studies, etc.
  • Startup company fundraiser to invest in the company for better working on the company goal.


Our mission is to connect people who donate the needful people and share happiness! FUNDZ’s vision for the future is “Fund Help for all”. We have not limited ourselves to Sports/Healthcare/Education/StartUp, we also plan to start Campaign and disaster relief funding too.
